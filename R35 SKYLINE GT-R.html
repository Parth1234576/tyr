Explore the history of the iconic sports car


The GT-R R35 was introduced in 2007 and is still in production today. It is a high-performance sports car that is known for its exceptional handling and performance. It is powered by a 3.8-liter twin-turbocharged V6 engine that produces up to 600 horsepower and 481 lb-ft of torque, and features a sophisticated all-wheel drive system that provides exceptional traction and stability.
The GT-R R35 has undergone several updates and improvements since its introduction, including a refresh in 2011, a Nismo version in 2014, and several performance and styling upgrades throughout its production run. It is widely regarded as one of the best sports cars of its time and has won numerous awards and accolades for its performance and engineering.
